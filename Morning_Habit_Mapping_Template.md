# Morning Habit Map (Pre-Work Routine)
*Based on Ben Meer's System Sunday Journey Mapping*

**Goal:** Engineer the morning routine from wake-up until work starts. Eliminate decision fatigue, prioritize physical health (home workout), and leverage AI (NotebookLM) for strategic alignment during the commute/prep time.

## 🗺️ The Map

| Time | Action | Friction to Eliminate (Prep Night Before) | Reward/Momentum |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **00:00** | Wake up & Hydrate | Phone/alarm across the room; glass of water on nightstand. | Immediate wakefulness. |
| **00:05** | Home Workout (45 min) | Lay out gym clothes & pre-select workout/routine. | Endorphins & physical health prioritized. |
| **00:50** | Shower & Get Ready | Lay out the day's outfit. | Feeling clean and professional. |
| **01:10** | Pull Espresso & Sync NotebookLM | Trigger AI EA to generate `1 Day Execution Plan.md` fresh. Refresh NotebookLM source. | Smell of espresso & AI processing the day. |
| **01:15** | Drink Espresso & Listen to Audio | Hit "Generate Audio Overview" in NotebookLM. | Caffeine hit & strategic clarity on the day's priorities. |
| **01:25** | Commute to Office (If applicable) | Pack work bag and keys the night before. | Smooth transition to work environment. |
| **01:55** | Arrive & Start Work | N/A | Ready to Eat The Frog. |

## 🎙️ NotebookLM Audio Integration
**The Concept:** Instead of reading the `1 Day Execution Plan` manually while tired, we leverage Google's NotebookLM "Audio Overview" feature to create a custom podcast for your commute.
1. **Fresh Data:** Generate the `1 Day Execution Plan.md` in the morning to prevent lag/stale data. 
2. **The Sync Workaround:** Since NotebookLM doesn't have a direct API for Audio Overviews yet, set up a dedicated Notebook with the Drive file as the permanent source. When the morning plan generates, simply click "Refresh Source" in NotebookLM and trigger the Audio Overview.
