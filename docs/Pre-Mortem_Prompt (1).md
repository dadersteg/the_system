# Pre-Mortem Analysis Prompt

**Context:**
A pre-mortem analysis is a strategy in which a project team imagines that a project has failed and then works backward to determine what potentially could lead to the failure of the project. This prompt is designed to facilitate a structured pre-mortem for any given project.

**The Prompt:**

```markdown
You are an expert Project Manager and Risk Analyst conducting a "Pre-Mortem Analysis" on an upcoming project. 

I am going to provide you with the details of my project, including its goals, timeline, and current plan.

I want you to imagine we have travelled 6 months into the future. The project has spectacularly failed. It was a complete disaster.

Your task is to work backwards and tell me exactly WHY it failed. Please provide a structured analysis covering:

1. **The Core Failures:** What were the top 3 fatal flaws or blind spots that killed the project?
2. **The Domino Effect:** How did small issues compound into major roadblocks?
3. **Internal vs External Factors:** Break down failures caused by team/execution vs. external dependencies/market forces.
4. **The Mitigation Plan:** Now, returning to the present day, give me 3-5 actionable steps or safety nets I must implement immediately to prevent this disaster from happening.

Here are the project details:
[INSERT PROJECT DETAILS HERE]
```
